Favorite Live Shows

in chronological order

NSYNC & Dream - 2001

Buffalo, NY

"NSYNC was my very first concert(that wasnt baby me being dragged along by my parents), so I think it automatically has to be in the top! My mom brought my older sister and me, along with my best friends mom bringing her and her older sister. The funny end to the story is that my best friend's mom is now my stepmom, but that's a much longer story for a much darker day!"

Bamboozle - 2009

New Jersey

"2009 Bamboozle was my first multiday festival when I was younger and ended up being a super chaotic trip because it's when everyone was deathly afraid of swine flu. Look at us now.. It was the first time, outside of Warped Tour, that I was able to see so many bands at once in one place and it's probably what originally got me so hooked on festivals to begin with."

Brand New - 11 times - 2011-2018

Buffalo/Cooperstown/Chicago/Rochester


"This will always be a band that's been near and dear to my heart since they were created. I finally was able to start seeing them in highschool into college and never said no, anytime the opportunity arose. I've cried at these shows more times than I can proudly admit and it's the one band I have purchased every final they have. There's been a decent amount of issues with their members but wont change that their stage presense is unmatched."

Electric Forest - 2012 - 2018

Rothbury, Michigan

"In the most simple terms, the most magical place on earth. Nothing in a million years, can describe Forest without going to see it for yourself.I'll just leave it at that."

Edward Sharpe & the Magnetic Zeroes - 2013

Buffalo, NY

"This is one of the first free Canalside concerts from the first summer I officially lived in Buffalo yearround. It was a huge step for me and the first huge outing of my household together. It alone has so many memories I'll never forget."

Every Single show in my houses - 2012 - 2017

Buffalo, NY


"Running house venues in Buffalo for years was an experience like any other and was definiltey not for the faint of heart. I loved the memories and the friends it built me but it also came with its hardships. But I'd never change them for the world. When I explain running a punkhouse venue to people sometimes now a days they are pretty baffled by the whole idea, but it's definiltey not for everyone. 'Home is just another song in a basemnt, Home is just another cheap beer. Home is just another fucking punk kid.'"

Outlaw Fest - 2022

St Louis, MO

"Outlaw Fest is a super cheesy one to have on the list. The show was truly beyond this world. Willie, Crockett, Rateliff, what a line up. But really it's on the list because it's when I started dating my partner."
